Holiday Schedule

Below are the available holiday schedules for Wayne State College and the Nebraska State College System. If the holiday falls on an employee’s regularly scheduled day off, the College will allow the employee to reschedule the observance date on an alternative day with prior authorization from the direct supervisor. For employees who are required to work on a holiday, comparable time off will be given per the appropriate union agreement or board policy.
