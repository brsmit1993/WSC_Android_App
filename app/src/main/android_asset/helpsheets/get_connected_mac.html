Get Connected - Mac

1. Click the Wifi icon in the menubar and select WSC-Secure.

2. Enter your WSC username and password. Click OK.

3. Select Trust if a window appears regarding a "certificate".

4. Enter your computer password to confirm.

You should now be connected to WSC-Secure.

 

Please contact the Campus Service Center at 402-375-7107 or visit us in the library if you are still having problems connecting.
