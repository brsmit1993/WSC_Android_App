Directory Information on WildcatsOnline

Federal law allows Wayne State to release "Directory Information" without written permission of the student. See FERPA guidelines for additional information about your privacy.

Any Wayne State student who wishes to have their Directory Information protected can do so through WildcatsOnline.

1. Go to the WSC home page, click on myWSC at the top, and log in.

2. Click on WildcatsOnline from the Top Applications section.

3. Select the Profile tab.

4. Click on Restrict All Fields in the FERPA section.

 

Note: You may also submit written notification to the Records & Registration Office.
