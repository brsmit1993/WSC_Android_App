Get Connected - iPhone or iPad

1. Open Settings.

2. Tap Wi-Fi.

3. Select WSC-Secure.

4. Enter your WSC username and password.

5. Tap Join.

6. You will now see Certificate information, tap Trust.

You should now be connected to WSC-Secure.

 

Please contact the Campus Service Center at 402-375-7107 or visit us in the library if you are still having problems connecting.
