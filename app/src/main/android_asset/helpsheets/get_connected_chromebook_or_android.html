Get Connected - Chromebook or Android Device

The information being provided is very generic due to the fact that the setup for connecting to a wireless network tends to vary based on the manufacturer of the Android device you own.  Please contact the the Campus Service Center at 402-375-7107 if you are unable to get connected.
 

1. Open Settings.
    a. You can do this either from your main screen by selecting your menu button and then Settings (System Settings) or by accessing the Settings app within your all Apps (App Tray/App Drawer)

2. Select Wi-Fi .
    a. If your Wi-Fi toggle is turned off, you will need to turn it on.

3. From the list of available networks, select WSC-Secure.

4. Under EAP method, select PEAP.

5. Under Phase 2 Authentication, select MSCHAPV2.

6. Under CA Certificate, select Do Not Validate.

7. Under Domain, type wsc.edu

8. Under Identity, enter your WSC username  (ex: josmit01).

9. If an Annonymous Identity field is provided, leave it blank.

10. Under Password, enter your WSC password.

11. Select Connect.

You should now be connected to WSC-Secure.

 

Please contact the Campus Service Center at 402-375-7107 or visit us in the library if you are still having problems connecting.
