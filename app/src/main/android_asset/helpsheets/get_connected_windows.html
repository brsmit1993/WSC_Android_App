Get Connected - Windows

1. Click on the Wifi icon at the bottom right-hand corner of the screen.

2. Select WSC-Secure from the list. Click Connect.

3. Enter your WSC username and password and click Connect.

You should now be connected to WSC-Secure.

Note: If your Windows device is a desktop connecting over Ethernet (rather than via WiFi), you will need to register your computer's Ethernet "physical" address, using our Device Registration portal, before connecting.

 

Please contact the Campus Service Center at 402-375-7107 or visit us in the library if you are still having problems connecting.
